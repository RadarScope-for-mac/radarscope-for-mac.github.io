RadarScope

Specialized display utility for weather enthusiasts and meteorologists. View real NEXRAD Level 3 radar data—reflectivity, velocity, and more—from sites across the United States, Guam, and Puerto Rico. Rendered in original radial format for unmatched detail. Track mesocyclones, hurricane eyewalls, velocity couplets, and severe weather warnings with precision on your Mac.

Key Advantages

True Radial Data

No smoothed images. Experience authentic Level 3 NEXRAD radar in its native format, revealing fine details like hook echoes and storm-relative velocity couplets.

Live Warnings

Monitor tornado, severe thunderstorm, and flash flood alerts from the National Weather Service. Browse active warnings in the sidebar, view details, and zoom instantly to affected areas.

Fluid Navigation

Zoom with pinch gestures or mouse, pan effortlessly, select from 155+ radar sites via map, list, or favorites. Animate up to 20 frames with one tap. Dynamic city labels appear as you explore.

Smart Automation

Data updates every 5–10 minutes based on radar strategy. Hover the legend for precise values. Export maps to clipboard or common image formats seamlessly.

Global Reach

Now with radars in Denmark, Australia (ABAP, AKRR, ATOO), and dual-pol products for AMAC. Built for macOS 10.14 through 11.

How It Works

1. Select Radar

Choose from 155+ NEXRAD sites on the map, sidebar list, or your saved favorites in the menu bar.

2. View and Navigate

Zoom in/out with mouse or trackpad gestures. Drag to pan. Display reflectivity, velocity, or other products in true radial format.

3. Monitor Warnings

Browse active severe weather alerts in the sidebar. Select one to read details and center the map on it.

4. Animate and Export

Tap play for looping animations. Copy the view to clipboard or save as an image. Updates arrive automatically.
